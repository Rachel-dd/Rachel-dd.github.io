GDW Exercise 3.4: Objectives

Exercise: List ten of your favorite games and name the objective for each. Do you see any similarities in these games? Try to define the type or types of games that appeal to you.

  1. Bloodborne
    Chase: you can speed up to chase a monster and also elude to reduce damage.
    Rescue or escape: Rescue the NPC as possible as i can.
    Exploration: explore Yanan.
    Solution: Somewhere we need to find out unnormal solution to go.
    Outwit: When I beat a monster that other players think is difficult.

  2. Castlevania
    Rescue or escape: Rescue the NPC as possible as i can. Escape from the castle.
    Exploration: explore everywhere in the castle.
    Solution: Somewhere we need to find out unnormal solution to go.

  3. Original Sin 2
    Capture: You can destroy of steal some items from the NPCs.
    Rescue or escape: Rescue the NPC as possible as i can.
    Forbidden Act: you can steal and you will get paid. The results depend on how you use these unsual ways.
    Construction: combine some items into a new one.
    Exploration: explore everywhere in the game.

  4. Fit Ring
    Chase: you can speed up your running to reach the destination quicker.
    Construction: combine some items into a new one.

  5. Learn while true
    Alignment: arrange the points to get better solution.
    Constrution: to build a well-work programme that meet the requirements.
    Solution: we always have many solutions for one task.

  6. Chinese Paladin
    Rescue or escape: Rescue the NPC
    Exploration: explore everywhere

  7. the sims
    Capture: You can destroy of steal some items from the NPCs.
    Forbidden act: MODS
    Construction: build house
    Exploration

  8. Jianxia3
    Capture
    Chase
    Race
    Rescue of escape
    Exploration
    Outwit

  9. human fall flat
    capture
    alignment
    rescue or escape
    construction
    exploration
    solution

  10. dark souls 3
    Chase: you can speed up to chase a monster and also elude to reduce damage.
    Rescue or escape: Rescue the NPC as possible as i can.
    Exploration: explore Yanan.
    Solution: Somewhere we need to find out unnormal solution to go.
    Outwit: When I beat a monster that other players think is difficult.

I like exploration, solution, rescue or escape games most.
