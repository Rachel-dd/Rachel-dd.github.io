GDW Exercise 4.8: Story

Exercise: Pick a game that you feel successfully melds its story line with the gameplay. Why does this game succeed? How does the plot unfold as the game progresses?

Undertale:
The story of the undertale is an adventure game and meta game. God, i like the meta game. The choice you made will be remembered by the game characters and influence them.
So I’m engaged in the game because i feel like i’m really the protagonist and i’m making friends with the game characters.
What kind of person i am, shaped the protagonist. And this influence the experience of my game play deeply and everywhere. I’m fucking liking the game.
