GDW Exercise 2.8: Story

Exercise: Have any stories within a game ever gripped you, moved you emotionally, or sparked your imagination? If so, why? If not, why not?

Life is strange:
The last choice of save Chole or save the city. I cried when i saw a UP playing the game at that scenario.
It was very difficult for her to chose save the city…So did I. We were immersed with the memories with Chole, and at that moment, we knew that we were going to lose her.
It was “right” to save the city. But how can we say it was “wrong” to save Chole?

What make this scenario so moving is the memories, music, emotion, and so on… We’ve spent many moments with Chole and she was a vivid person. We can’t stand to lose her.
But we’re also faced with billiant innocent people’s life. The difficult situation is impressive for us. And the goodbye kiss also killed us.
