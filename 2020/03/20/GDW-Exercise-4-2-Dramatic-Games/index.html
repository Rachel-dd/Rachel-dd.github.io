GDW Exercise 4.2: Dramatic Games

Exercise: Name five games that you find dramatically interesting. What is it about those games that you find compelling?

  1. Original Sin2
    You can do everything you can think in this game: love, steal, fight without words, fly… Even you think many ways to accomplish the task, there still be more solutions.
    During the solving problems, you know more about the world and get more immersed in it. The script in this game have variety styles: homourous, smart, elegant, funny… which build different shapes of charaters.

  2. BABA IS YOU
    You can change the rule to accomplish your task. You can not only be BABA

  3. YAKUZA
    A good story teller with interesting small games, showing us a ture old Jpan

  4. Life is strange
    No matter what choice you made before, you will be faced with the difficulties question at last…

  5. Uncharted Waters 4
    I feel i like a chaptain. The most interesting thing for me is to explore the world, espacially where i havn’t and have been to in reality.(That means every where is exciting!)
