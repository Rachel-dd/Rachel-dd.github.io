GDW Exercise 2.6: Challenge

Exercise: Name three games that you find particularly challenging and describe why.

  1. Bloodborne
    The first soullike game I ever played. The enemy are stronger than you at the beginning(huge amount and high attack), so it’s hard to meet the first boss at the beginning.
    Even when you pass the 1st boss, It’s very hard to live in that game and you’re easily get frustrated when you died again and again. Your life is limited and the resources are limited too.
    If you continue to chanllenge the monsters, your win rate will become higher and higher. The difficulties boss I used 4 h to defeat it.
    But that challenge will make you more happy when you defeat it. That’s me.

  2. Original sin 2
    I like to challenge games so i chose the Highest difficulty at the beginning. And no doubt, i was beaten into shit. The enemy is much stronger than you, more life, more skills and more damages.
    But when you win a battle by trying all different ways, you learn more about the battle. And this will be your next weapon.

  3. Sekiro
    Even the Sekiro is produced by From Software and i’m a big fan of dark souls, i don’t like Sekiro.
    The challenge of Sekiro is that you can only win the enemy in limited ways, unlike the dark soul. If you want to win, you must learn to defend in that game. But i don’t like defend.
    So I buy this game to support Miyazaki Eita but i didn’t go trough Sekiro.
