GDW Exercise 4.10: Plotting a Story, Part 2

Exericse: Now take the same game and plot the gameplay against the dramatic arc.

What elements of gamepley, if any, support each of these points?
How is the exposition of gameplay handled? Are controls and mechanics clearly explained? Are they intergrated with the dramatc premise? Is the goal clearly stated and intergrated
with the main conflict of the story?
How does the gameplay cause the dramatic tension to rise?
What deciding factor in the gameplay brings the game to a climax?
What happend in the resolution? Do the dramatic elements and gamplay elements help or hinder each other?
How might they be better intergrated to make the game work from an emotional standpoint?

  1. the skill related checkpoint, the time, the equipment, the consuming item all support these points.
  2. The skill(which can be enhanced by equipment and item) affect the failrate of the checkpoint> the checkpoint goes to different talks > might lead to different solutions.
    The controls and mechanics are clearly explained, and they are integrated with the dramatic premise. The goal is clearly stated and integrated.
  3. The gameplay always play an important role when you are on the way to the climax. By the way, the game is more like linear game, but different choices made by your strong or
    weak skills will lead to some different results. The climax is always there when you are reaching some point, but the performance is a liitle bit different and you can experience different emotions.
  4. The deciding factor … Mainly focus on how you know about the case- like how many talks have you unlocked, have you collect enough imformation? When you got enough imformation, you will reach to the next climax.
  5. In the resolution, some part of importance truth is revealed and the protagonist always gain something(bullet, injuries… memories.. love…). The elements sometimes help, sometimes hinder the things you get in the resolution. All depends on your choice
  6. The game is ferfect. The elements of those no-use items can be enhanced maybe
