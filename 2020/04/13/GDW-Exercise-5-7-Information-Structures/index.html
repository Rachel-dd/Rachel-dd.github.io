GDW Exercise 5.7: Information Structures

Exercise: What types of information structures are present in Unreal Tournament, Age of Empires, Jak II, Madden 2004, Lemmings, Scrabble, Mastermind, and Clue?
Do they have open, hidden, mixed, or dymanic information structures? If you do not know one of the games, pick a game that i have not mentioned and substitute it.

Unreal Tournament: open

Age of Empires: Mixed or Dynamic-fog of war

Jak II: Dynamic or Mixed

Madden 2004: Open

Lemmings: Mixed

Scrabble: Open

Mastermind: Open

Clue: Mixed
